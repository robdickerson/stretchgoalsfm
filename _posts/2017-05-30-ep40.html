In this episode of the Stretch Goals podcast, Scott and I are going to be asking you the question are you a micromanager?

You might be a micro manager if:

  • You look over their shoulders while they work
  • You ask for daily status reports
  • You have endless meetings for the sake of keeping track
  • You don't trust your employees to work from home
