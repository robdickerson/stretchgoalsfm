In this episode, we'll be talking about 4 common traits of an entrepreneur referenced from this article Hiring An Entrepreneurial Leader.

  • Entrepreneurs are curious seekers of adventure, learning, and opportunity.
  • Entrepreneurs are more comfortable with risk.
  • Entrepreneurs are driven by a need to own products, projects, and initiatives.
  • Entrepreneurs are natural salespeople.
